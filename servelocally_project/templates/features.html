ServeLocally is built to bring your entire community together and match volunteers with needs.

It combines great technology into an open source system that is easy to customize for your community.

For Volunteers

  • Do you like preparing food? Tutoring? Subscribe just to the areas you're interested in and get customized results and notifications
  • Choose which notifications to receive
  • No internet access? You can search from your phone, by dialing, or text message
  • No special account needed to see opportunities or volunteer
  • Track your community service work, and set goals

For Organizations

  • Post openings easily
  • Track availability
  • Automatically have your postings available for distribution through the web, SMS, and telephone searches
  • Easily approve volunteer hours, for a validated log.
  • Post events, or ongoing needs

For Groups

Boy Scout Troops, Classrooms, Businesses and more

  • Track your group's hours
  • Work together towards a common Goal
  • Easily Share other organization's needs with your entire group and volunteer together
