ServeLocally is an Open Source project on Github.

Even though the code is easily accessible, there's a lot more to getting an active volunteer matching service up and running in your community than just running the website.

Send us an Email at info@servelocally.org and we'll help you get on your way.

Developer Documentation is served by ReadTheDocs at http://docs.servelocally.org/

There is also a mailing list on Librelist. You can subscribe by sending an email to servelocally@librelist.org.
